About Art Liberation

Art Liberation is an NPO which focuses on supporting SMEs that sell art such as paintings, clothes, sculptures and etc which are found to be the most difficult things to sell in South Africa. Therefore, Art Liberation provides free advertising using social media platform such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest and YouTube videos for people who might be interested in these SMEs products.

According to the research Art Liberation conducted, it has found that artists/entrepreneurs who own art SMEs are trying to liberate or rather free themselves from poverty, substance abuse, bad decision making and so on. So, Art Liberation creates a platform to help expose their products to the world, improve their business Public Relations, Marketing skills and help them get funding for their businesses.

VISION

Our vision is to improve the standard of living of our brother's and sister's who sell art for a living in South Africa. By making sure that they are noticed by people across the world and exposed to opportunities which can lead to the improvement of their businesses.

MISSION

Our mission is to help all the art SMEs in South Africa who need our help in improving their PR and marketing communication skills so that they can be recognized by big brands and people across the globe.
